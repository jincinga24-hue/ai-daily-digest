# Validation Report

## Idea
The Daily AI Shift — A daily auto-generated AI news blog tracking industry impact with an interactive radar chart and curated resource feed.

## MVP Features
1. Daily Blog Digest
2. AI Industry Impact Radar (interactive spider chart)
3. Learn & Adapt Resource Feed (curated external links)

## Success Metric
All 3 pages fully functional, mobile responsive, data-driven from JSON files. User can browse posts, explore radar chart, and click through curated resources.

## Kill Metric
If after 10 cycles the blog page can't render posts from JSON and the radar chart doesn't display, pivot approach.

## Validation
- Validated by user discussion
- Clear use case: student tracking AI career impact
- Unique angle: connecting daily AI news to specific career fields dynamically
